Maintaining a minimum level of text contrast is critical for the usability and accessibility or your sites and applications.

Following the WCAG accessibility guidelines ensures that the majority of users will be able to read and understand your text content, whether they're colorblind, have poor vision, or are just reading off of a screen with glare from the sun.
